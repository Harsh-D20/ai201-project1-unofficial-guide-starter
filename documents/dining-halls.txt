Dining Halls

Students with Anytime Dining access can come into any dining hall, any time we are open and enjoy any amount of anything we're serving -- any number of times per day.
Dining halls accept Anytime Dining Plans, Connector Dining Plans, credit and debit cards, Dining Dollars and Terrapin Express.

During the semester, times for breakfast, lunch and dinner are:
- Breakfast: Open to 10:30am
- Lunch: 10:30am to 4:00pm
- Dinner:  4:00pm to Close
