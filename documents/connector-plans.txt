Connector Dining Plans

Connector Dining Plans & Block Meal Plans:

UMD Dining offers flexible dining plans for students who live in a space with their own kitchen! These plans are designed to provide students with access to healthy, delicious meals while connecting them to the UMD community in welcoming spaces. Here's an overview of the options:

Block Meal Plans
- Flexibility for Off-Campus Students : Block Meal Plans are a great choice for off-campus students who want the ability to dine on campus without committing to unlimited dining. These plans provide a preset number of meals per semester. Meals can be used at any of the dining halls, giving students the freedom to choose when and where they eat.
- Fall to Spring Roll Over : Unused meals on your Block Plan will roll over from the Fall to the Spring semester, and all unused meals expire when dining halls close at the end of Spring Semester Finals Week.

Summer Dining Plans
- We are open during the summer! Dining hall hours and menus change daily based on the groups who have booked meals on campus, Dining in the Stamp, the South Campus Market and many of the Cafes on Campus will be open with shortened hours. Schedules post four weeks in advance. Visit dining.umd.edu/hours-locations .
- Meals - 10 meals for $105. Use meals in any dining hall and enjoy fresh-cooked meals on campus!
- Dining Dollars - purchase at a discount, spend Dollar for dollar in any Dining Services location.
- Meals and Dining Dollars roll over summer to fall to spring and expire when dining halls close at the end of the Spring Semester.

Anytime Dining Plans
- Unlimited Access : For students who want unlimited access to all three dining halls, the Anytime Dining Plan offers the most flexibility. You can dine in any of the dining halls any time during the week and enjoy as many meals as you like. Students who live in traditional on-campus housing participate in the Seven Day Dining Plan.
- Five-Day & Seven-Day Plans : The Five-Day Plan offers unlimited access during weekdays, allowing you to cook at home or elsewhere on weekends.

Dining Dollars
- If you don’t want a full dining plan but still want a discount, you can purchase Dining Dollar bundles . These bundles provide a 5% discount , plus you save 6% sales tax , and they spend Dollar-for-dollar. Dining Dollars are available in bundles that can be used at any Dining Services location. These bundles roll over from Fall to Spring and expire at the end of finals week, Spring Semester.
- You can purchase up to two Dining Dollar bundles per semester. Please note that Dining Dollars are non-refundable .

JHH Breakfast Plan

The JHH Breakfast Plan provides unlimited access to a breakfast buffet at the Jones Hill Team House, home of the Terrapin Football Team, Monday through Friday. This plan excludes University holidays.

Important Information
- Plan Sign Up : To sign up for any of these dining plans, log into your Dining Account . This will give you access to all the benefits and flexibility of Maryland Dining, from delicious meals to convenient dining experiences.
- Connector Plans and Dining Dollars carry over Fall to Spring and are not refundable .
- Five-Day Plans and Connector Block Meal Plans do not fulfill the Resident Dining Plan requirement for students living in traditional on-campus housing.
- Plan Expiry : All Connector Plans and Dining Dollars expire at the end of Spring Semester , so be sure to use them before the end of the academic year.

Log in to Dining Account to sign up for a plan, and start enjoying the quality, convenience, and hospitality of Maryland Dining.
